GiveItForward

Connecting generous donors with people who truly need help

About the Service

GiveItForward is a simple charity platform where people can donate unused items, and people in need can request them. Our goal is to reduce waste, support communities, and make it easier for everyone to help each other.

Donate Items

If you have clothes, electronics, toys, or household items you no longer use, you can offer them here. Your donation can make a real difference.

Offer an Item

Request Items

If you or someone you care for is in need, you can submit a request. We match requests with available items donated by the community.

Request Help

How It Works

  • Donors list the items they want to give.
  • People in need submit their requests.
  • We match both sides and arrange safe collection or delivery.
